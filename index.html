Armand Gissler

I am a PhD student in mathematics in the Randopt Inria team, located at CMAP, École polytechnique. My supervisors are Anne Auger and Nikolaus Hansen.

Research interests

Currently, I work on the algorithm CMA-ES, and theoretical fundations for convergence. I am also interested in topics around Markov chains theory.
